HopNet

Private, end-to-end-encrypted messaging — for the people you actually know.

HopNet keeps your conversations strictly between you and the people you choose. Every message is end-to-end encrypted, so no one in the middle — not even us — can read it. You add contacts in person, by scanning their QR code, so there is no random matching and no stranger chat.

Download on the App Store

Why HopNet

  • End-to-end encryption — X3DH key agreement and the Double Ratchet, using standard, audited algorithms. Only you and your contact can read your messages.
  • No phone number, no email, no account — your identity is a key that lives only on your phone.
  • You choose every contact — added in person by QR code and verified with safety numbers. HopNet never matches you with strangers.
  • Built for safety — a zero-tolerance policy for abuse, with report and block tools in every chat. See our Terms & Community Standards.
  • You're in control — block or report anyone, and wipe all your data instantly from Settings.

Support

Privacy

HopNet has no accounts and collects no personal information. Read the full Privacy Policy to see exactly how messages travel and what the offline relay can (and cannot) see.
